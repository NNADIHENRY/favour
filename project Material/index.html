Abia State University Uturu Journal of Architecture,Technology,Engineering and

Environmental Studies (ABSUJATEES) is published twice a 

year under the Platform of the research team of the Faculty of Environmental Studies of

 the University. 

The aim of the Journal is to encourage academicians and students to publish their

 research(experimental 

and theoretical)details. The Journal provides an advanced Knowledge platform for 

Architects, Engineers, Environmental Managers, Urban and Regional Planners,

 Geography and planning, Builders, Estate Management, 

Quantity Surveyors, land surveyors and project Management Professsionals

. Abia state University Uturu Journals 

of Architecture,Technology,Engineering and Environmental Studies

 is an institutional based (ABSUJATEES) journal with open access to peer

 reviewed hardcover and online publications;which presents original research articles.

 The primary objective of the

 publications is to provide a primer portal of high quality research work in 

architecture and the built environment.

Periodicity:

Abia State University Uturu Journal of  

Architecture and the Built Environment

(ABSUUJABE) is published once every

 six months that is two issues annually.
